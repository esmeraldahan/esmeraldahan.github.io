Protect Venice

Venice may be facing an existential threat, but there are steps that can be taken to protect this extraordinary city from sinking into the sea. One key approach is to focus on reducing the impact of human activity on the surrounding environment.

This means adopting sustainable practices and technologies that limit pollution, carbon emissions, and other factors that contribute to global warming and sea level rise.

Another important strategy is to invest in infrastructure and engineering solutions that can help mitigate the effects of rising tides and flooding.

Such measures may be costly and require significant investment, but they offer the best chance of safeguarding Venice's unique cultural heritage for future generations.
